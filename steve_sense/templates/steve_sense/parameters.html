Climate Control

Lighting

'Light On' specifies what time the light comes on.

'Duration' dictates for how long the light will remain on.


The 'Intensity Timing Pattern' is created by entering a minimum of 2 Time & intensity(%) values


The 'Auto-Sync Light Rail' feature will start the light rail when the light is first powered and scale the speed until it reaches its end-point when the light goes off.

Use the controls to add points.

'Rail Start' specifies what time the light rail will start moving.

'Duration' dictates how long it should take to reach its end-point.
